Frequently asked questions

Do you visit patients at home?

Yes. Home visits are available for anyone who finds travel difficult. Just mention it when you book.

What should I prepare before a visit?

A comfortable chair and good lighting is helpful. Please remove nail polish if possible.

How long does an appointment take?

Most sessions last 30-45 minutes depending on your needs.
